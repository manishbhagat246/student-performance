Dataloader File

A dataloader is a crucial element in predicting academic performance, particularly in math, reading, and writing. It acts as a link between raw data and predictive models, efficiently loading and organizing datasets for analysis. The dataloader plays a vital role in managing diverse data sources like student records and socio-economic factors, ensuring the predictive model has access to a well-structured dataset. Its integration provides valuable insights into factors influencing academic success, enabling proactive interventions and personalized support. This fosters a data-driven approach in education, enhancing student learning outcomes across subjects.

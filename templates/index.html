Welcome to Student Performance Prediction

Explore our platform that bridges data and education, offering a comprehensive view of high school students' performance in mathematics. Our dataset, sourced from three distinguished high schools across the United States, provides valuable insights into the factors influencing student outcomes.

Discover the dynamics of academic achievement through key columns such as Gender, Race/Ethnicity, Parental Level of Education, Lunch, Test Preparation Course, and Math, Reading, and Writing Scores. Whether you're a researcher, educator, or enthusiast, our dataset serves as a rich resource for exploring diverse aspects of education.

From analyzing the impact of parental education to developing machine learning models predicting student performance, our platform opens doors to a world of data-driven insights. Join us on this journey as we pave the way for a brighter educational future.
